Our History

Nxt Museum is the first museum in the Netherlands dedicated to new media art. We focus on art that uses modern tools to embody modern times. we believe that the tools used in artistic expression reflect the times we live in they provide the perfect vehicle for understanding today's complexities, allowing us to do just that recognize, relate and reflect on our reality.

What Is NXT?

Nxt Museum is the first museum in the Netherlands that focuses entirely on media art. We exhibit works of art that use modern technologies of the present and of the future. Because art reflects the spirit of the times, we believe that the most innovative art gives us the opportunity to assess the impact of technological developments to reflect. A moment of reflection in a reality that is developing ever faster.

Our Mission

Our mission is to stimulate progressive forms of creativity. We see the future as one of the most fascinating concepts of the human brain. It is an inexhaustible source of inspiration that holds us in its grip and raises the question: Whats Nxt?. In view of technological and social developments, this question is a greater mystery than ever before. Will you join us in looking for the answer?
